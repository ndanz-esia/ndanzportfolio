About Me

Let Me Introduce My Self

Hamdan D. Prasetya, who is familiarly called Ndanz, is a graphic designer who lives in Surabaya. Born in Nganjuk Regency, East Java, I have been involved in the world of graphic design as a freelancer, and have obtained professional certification in this field.

I am very interested in everything artistic. Apart from graphic design, I also have skills in writing, drawing, editing and photography. I try to produce creative and innovative works that reflect my love of art. You can find some of my work here.
