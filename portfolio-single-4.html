Draft Aksarahayu

Project Description

Combining the beauty of the characters with the peace implied in the name Rahayu, this collection of poetry explores romance, pain and solace in life's tortuous journey. In each stanza, the author reveals his once hidden feelings for a close friend which slowly turn into love. Aksarahayu is not just a collection of poetry; This is a mirror of the soul that illustrates how complicated human emotions can be when logic and feelings are triggered.
